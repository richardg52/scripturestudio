How It Works

Your voice matters.
We built the room for it.

No camera, no pressure. Just a live audio conversation with real ministry leaders led by Dr. Clark.

1

Enter the Room

Click "Enter the Room" on session day. You'll join muted by default — no video, just audio.

2

Raise Your Hand

Tap the raise hand button when you want to speak or ask a question. Dr. Clark sees your request live.

3

Host Unmutes You

Dr. Clark calls your name and unmutes you directly. You speak, the room hears you — then you return to muted.

4

Send a Question

Can't speak? Type your question in the chat panel. Dr. Clark reads and answers selected questions live.
